About Me

Hello stranger! You have just visited my website and you are wondering what it is about? Well, this is my personal portfolio. My (User)name is lunAr-creator. Here you will get to know how I started coding and what I have made, including some of my UI (concept) contributions to Project Vanguard. Furthermore, you will be able to view my most recent projects and tinker with my code!

I have HTML, CSS, and Javascript skills from Highschool, including scratch and C# which got me started with programming. My weapon of choice at the moment is Python, which I have been learning for a large part of the lockdown.

During this time, I have made use of all the spare time i have had and sat down and programmed for hours on end. Needless to say, i think it was very fruitful. I have made a UI for a calculator, made some text-based games, solved some problems and made my first python library.

After the Ddosing issues surrounding Titanfall 1 and 2 in recent months, I happened to find Project Vanguard on reddit, the community led 'Titanfall 3'. I love designing things and of course, I love Titanfall, so applied for a UI Developer position so that I could help out.
